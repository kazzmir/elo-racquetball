Data Source

All match results and player profiles used on this site come from proracquetballstats.com, a comprehensive historical database of professional racquetball tournament results.

How ELO Is Calculated

Ratings use the FIDE ELO formula with the following K-factors:

  • K = 40 — fewer than 30 career games
  • K = 20 — 30+ games and ELO below 2400
  • K = 10 — ELO 2400 or above

All players start at ELO 1000.

Age Adjustment

To account for the natural advantage of younger players, each player's effective ELO is adjusted when computing expected match outcomes: for every 200 days a player is older than their opponent, their effective ELO is reduced by 1. This adjustment only affects the expected-score calculation — stored ELO values are always the true, unadjusted ratings. Birth dates come from player profiles on proracquetballstats.com; players with no listed birth date are assumed to have been 20 years old at the time of their first recorded match.
